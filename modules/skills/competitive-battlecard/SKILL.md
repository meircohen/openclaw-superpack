# Competitive Battlecard

Create actionable, sales-ready battlecards for positioning against specific competitors.

## Battlecard Structure

### 1. Quick Overview
```
Competitor: [Name]
Category: [Market segment]
Founded: [Year] | HQ: [Location] | Funding/Revenue: [Known info]
Target Customer: [Who they sell to]
Pricing: [Model and approximate range]
```

### 2. Positioning Statement
One paragraph: How WE differ from THEM in terms of value delivered.

### 3. Strengths vs. Weaknesses

| Their Strengths | Our Counter |
|----------------|-------------|
| [What they do well] | [Why our approach is better or equivalent] |

| Their Weaknesses | Our Advantage |
|-----------------|---------------|
| [Where they fall short] | [How we solve this] |

### 4. Common Objections + Responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Competitor X has feature Y" | "We solve the same problem with [approach], which also gives you [benefit]" |
| "They're cheaper" | "Our total cost of ownership is lower because [reason]" |
| "They're bigger/more established" | "We deliver [specific advantage] that large vendors can't" |

### 5. Killer Questions
Questions for sales to ask prospects that expose competitor weaknesses:
- "How does [competitor] handle [area where they're weak]?"
- "What happens when you need [capability we have, they don't]?"
- "Have you evaluated the total cost including [hidden cost]?"

### 6. Win/Loss Patterns
- **We win when**: [Buyer profile, use case, evaluation criteria]
- **We lose when**: [Conditions that favor competitor]
- **Key differentiator**: [The one thing that tips deals]

## Research Process
1. Check competitor's website, pricing page, changelog
2. Read G2/Capterra reviews (filter for recent, verified)
3. Search for "[competitor] vs" comparisons
4. Review their job postings for product direction signals
5. Check their GitHub/open-source activity if applicable
6. Monitor their social media and blog for messaging changes

## Maintenance
- Review and update quarterly
- Flag when competitor ships major features
- Track win/loss rate changes after battlecard deployment
