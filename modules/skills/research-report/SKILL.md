# Research Report

## Overview

Provides systematic methodology for conducting thorough research and creating structured reports with validated sources and synthesized insights.

## When to Use This Skill

**Use when:**
- Company research and competitive analysis
- Market landscape investigation 
- Industry trend analysis
- Investment research and due diligence support
- Strategic planning research
- Technology or product research requiring depth
- Any research requiring source validation and synthesis

**Don't use when:**
- Simple fact-checking or quick lookups
- Well-known information easily accessible
- Time-sensitive queries requiring immediate answers
- Personal research without business application
- Research where sources are already curated/provided

## Research Workflow

### 1. Research Planning
**Define scope and objectives:**
- Research question or hypothesis
- Required depth and timeline
- Key areas to investigate
- Success criteria for completeness

### 2. Information Gathering
**Systematic search strategy:**
- Primary web search across multiple queries
- Industry reports and expert sources
- Company financial filings and presentations
- News and recent developments
- Competitive intelligence gathering

### 3. Source Validation
**Credibility assessment:**
- Source authority and expertise
- Publication date and relevance
- Cross-reference with other sources
- Bias detection and mitigation
- Fact verification where possible

### 4. Synthesis & Analysis
**Information integration:**
- Pattern identification across sources
- Key insight extraction
- Gap identification and significance
- Trend analysis and implications
- Conflicting information reconciliation

### 5. Artifact Creation
**Structured deliverable:**
- Executive summary with key findings
- Detailed analysis with supporting evidence
- Source bibliography with credibility notes
- Recommendations or implications
- Areas for further investigation

## Quality Standards

### Research Depth
- **Multiple source types**: News, reports, financial data, expert analysis
- **Recent information**: Emphasis on last 12-24 months for relevance
- **Primary sources**: Direct company communications, filings, presentations
- **Expert perspectives**: Industry analyst views, thought leadership

### Source Validation
- **Authority verification**: Author/publication credibility
- **Bias awareness**: Commercial interests, opinion vs fact
- **Cross-validation**: Multiple sources for key claims
- **Currency check**: Information freshness and relevance

### Synthesis Quality
- **Pattern recognition**: Trends across multiple data points
- **Insight generation**: Beyond summary to analysis
- **Evidence support**: Claims backed by specific sources
- **Balanced perspective**: Acknowledgment of uncertainties/limitations

## Routing Logic

**Use market analysis template when:**
