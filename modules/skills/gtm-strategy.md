# Go-to-Market Strategy

Build a structured GTM strategy covering positioning, channels, messaging, and launch timeline.

## GTM Framework

### 1. Market Definition
- **Target segment**: Who is the ideal buyer? (Industry, size, role)
- **Problem**: What urgent pain are you solving?
- **Alternatives**: What do they use today? (Competitor, manual process, nothing)
- **Why now**: What makes this the right time?

### 2. Positioning (Geoffrey Moore Template)
```
For [target customer]
Who [statement of need/opportunity]
[Product name] is a [product category]
That [key benefit/reason to buy]
Unlike [primary competitive alternative]
Our product [primary differentiation]
```

### 3. GTM Motion Selection

| Motion | Best When | Key Metric |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Product-Led Growth | Low price, try-before-buy | Activation rate |
| Sales-Led | High ACV, complex sale | Pipeline velocity |
| Community-Led | Developer/creator tools | Community growth |
| Channel/Partner | Need distribution reach | Partner-sourced revenue |
| Content-Led | Education-heavy market | Organic traffic |

### 4. Channel Strategy
Rank channels by: reach, cost, speed, fit

| Channel | Priority | Budget | Expected CAC |
|---------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Content/SEO | High | Low | $X |
| Paid search | Medium | Medium | $Y |
| Outbound sales | High | High | $Z |

### 5. Messaging Matrix

| Audience | Pain Point | Message | CTA |
|----------|-----------|---------|-----|
| Technical buyer | [pain] | [how we solve] | Start free trial |
| Economic buyer | [pain] | [ROI story] | Book demo |
| End user | [pain] | [daily benefit] | Try it now |

### 6. Launch Timeline

| Week | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| -4 | Positioning finalized | Marketing | Messaging doc |
| -3 | Landing page + demo | Product | Live page |
| -2 | Content seeding | Content | Blog posts, social |
| -1 | Outreach to early adopters | Sales | Email sequences |
| 0 | Launch day | All | Announcement |
| +1 | Iterate on feedback | Product | v1.1 plan |

### 7. Success Metrics
- Awareness: Website traffic, social mentions
- Acquisition: Signups, demo requests, trials
- Activation: Time-to-value, feature adoption
- Revenue: MRR, pipeline, win rate
- Retention: Churn, NPS, expansion revenue
